Good to know

Pre-stadium dining FAQs

How far is Bar Franco from One NZ Stadium (Te Kaha)?

We're in The Crossing on Cashel Street, a short walk from One NZ Stadium (Te Kaha) in central Christchurch — ChristchurchNZ lists us as about a five-minute stroll. Easy to eat with us and wander over with time to spare.

What time should we eat before an event?

We open daily from 4pm, with the kitchen from 5pm. For a relaxed pre-event dinner, arriving around two hours before kick-off or doors is the sweet spot. On busy event nights, booking ahead is the safest bet.

Do you take bookings on event nights?

Yes — book a table online or call us on 03 925 9208. Concert and game nights are our busiest, so reserve early to lock in your table before the event.

Can we come back after the game or concert?

Absolutely. Our Spritz & Negroni Bar runs till late, so swing back in for a post-match drink or a nightcap after the final whistle.

Is it good for a group heading to the stadium?

Very. We seat groups across two levels and can put together a shared menu so a big crew can eat well and quickly. For larger bookings, get in touch about our group and function options.
