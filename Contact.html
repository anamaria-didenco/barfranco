Good to know

Visiting Bar Franco

Where is Bar Franco?

In The Crossing at 166 Cashel Street, Christchurch Central 8011 — right in the heart of the CBD.

What are your opening hours?

Open daily — the bar from 4pm and the kitchen from 5pm, both till late.

Is there parking nearby?

Yes — there's parking in The Crossing car park, plus plenty of central-city parking a short walk away.

How far is Bar Franco from One NZ Stadium?

A short walk — see our pre-stadium dining page for eating with us before the game or a concert.

How do I book or get in touch?

Book a table online or call 03 925 9208 (ciao@barfranco.nz). For events and venue hire, email anamaria@barfranco.nz or call 021 083 75416.
