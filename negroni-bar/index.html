The ritual

The best Negroni in Christchurch (we'd argue)

Equal parts gin, Campari and vermouth, stirred down and served with a twist — the Negroni is the icon of the occasion, and the house ritual at Bar Franco. It's the drink the whole place is named around.

Not in a bitter mood? There's the Aperol or Campari spritz, classic cocktails done properly, prosecco, a tidy Italian wine list, and low- and no-alcohol options for the driver. Aperitivo, the Italian way.

Good to know

Bar FAQs

Do I need to book for the bar?

Walk-ins are welcome at the bar downstairs — pull up a stool. If you'd like to dine, or you're bringing a group, it's best to book a table so we can look after you.

What's your signature drink?

The Negroni — it's the house ritual. Alongside it: Aperol and Campari spritzes, classic cocktails, prosecco, a tidy Italian wine list, and low- and no-alcohol options.

What time do you open?

Daily from 4pm — aperitivo o'clock — and we pour till late. The kitchen upstairs opens from 5pm.

Can we come just for drinks?

Absolutely. The bar's yours for a spritz, a Negroni or a glass of Italian wine — no dinner required. Stay for a plate of cicchetti if the mood takes you.

Do you do cocktails for groups and functions?

We do — from a stand-up cocktail function to the whole venue. Have a look at private events & venue hire.
