Good to know

Wedding FAQs

Can we have both our ceremony and reception here?

Yes — say your vows on one level and celebrate on the other, all under one roof. No travel between venues, no awkward gap. We'll plan the flow of your day with you.

How many guests can you host for a wedding?

Up to 240 across both levels for a standing reception, or around 165 seated. A single level is perfect for intimate weddings and elopements.

Is there a venue hire fee?

No venue hire fee. We work to a food and beverage minimum spend instead, tailored to your date, guest numbers and the spaces you use.

Can you cater dietaries and a wedding menu?

Absolutely — vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free and most allergies are no problem. We build a shared Italian menu around your guests, with seated menus from $80 per person.

When should we enquire?

As early as you can — prime weekend dates book out well ahead. Send your date and we'll let you know straight away if it's available.
