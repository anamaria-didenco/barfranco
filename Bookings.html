Good to know

Booking FAQs

Does Bar Franco take walk-ins?

Always — the bar downstairs keeps stools for walk-ins. For a dinner table, especially Thursday to Saturday or for a group, booking ahead is the safe bet.

How do I book a table?

Use the live availability widget on this page, or call us on 03 925 9208. For 10+ guests or full venue hire, our functions team will tailor the night.

Can you cater dietary requirements?

Yes — vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free and most allergies are no trouble. Just note it in your booking or tell your server.

What are your opening hours?

Open daily — the bar from 4pm and the kitchen from 5pm, till late.

Is there parking nearby?

We're in The Crossing at 166 Cashel Street, with parking in The Crossing car park and plenty of central-city parking close by.
