Good to know

Aperitivo FAQs

What time is aperitivo at Bar Franco?

Our Cinque a Sette aperitivo runs 5 to 7 nightly, and the bar is open daily from 4pm, till late. Drop in after work for a spritz and a bite before dinner upstairs.

Do I need to book?

No — walk in and pull up a stool at the Spritz & Negroni Bar. If you're bringing a group or would like to stay for dinner, it's best to book a table.

What's on the aperitivo board?

Cicchetti and Italian bar snacks to go with your drink — olives, focaccia, arancini and salumi. Ask what's on tonight, or see the drinks menu for spritzes, Negronis and wines by the glass.

Where is the aperitivo bar?

Downstairs at Bar Franco, in The Crossing at 166 Cashel Street, right in the heart of the Christchurch CBD.
