01 — ESSENCE

What Franco is

A modern Christchurch bar-restaurant that takes the feeling of Italian hospitality — generous, warm, a little theatrical — and strips out the kitsch.

If Franco were a person: confident but unfussy, warm, quick with a joke, impeccably dressed with the sleeves rolled up. A host, not a maître d'. Serious about the Negroni — never serious about itself.

03 — LOGO USAGE

Treating the marks well

Do
  • Give the wordmark clear space — at least the height of the "F" on every side.
  • Use red on cream/paper, or white reversed out of red or charcoal.
  • Let the dog and Negroni appear as playful accents — in a corner, peeking, walking.
  • Keep the marks crisp and at a legible size.
Don't
  • Recolour the marks outside the palette, add gradients, shadows or outlines.
  • Stretch, rotate or squash the wordmark.
  • Crowd the logo or place it on a busy photo without a scrim.
  • Let the dog or Negroni outshout the wordmark — they support, never lead.
