Gift card FAQs

How do I buy a Bar Franco gift card?

Buy online and it's delivered instantly by email, or pick one up in person next time you're in.

What can a gift card be used for?

Anything at Bar Franco — dinner upstairs, drinks at the bar, a long-table celebration. Treat it like cash toward the night.

Can I send one as a gift?

Yes — buy online and have it emailed straight to the lucky recipient, or grab a physical one in person.

Do gift cards expire?

Expiry and full terms are shown at checkout when you buy online, so you'll know exactly what you're giving.

Can I use it for a function or event?

Absolutely — put it toward a group booking or event. Just mention it when you get in touch with our events team.
