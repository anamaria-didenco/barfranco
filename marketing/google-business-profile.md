## 7. The review move that unlocks "event venue" rankings

Your 50+ reviews are gold — but Google reads the **words inside them** to decide what you're relevant for. If they mostly say "great pasta," Google keeps thinking *restaurant*, not *venue*.

**Ask event/function guests specifically to mention the occasion.** A short text or email after their event:

> "Ciao [Name]! It was so good hosting your [work Christmas party / 30th / engagement] at Franco. If you have 30 seconds, a quick Google review mentioning your event would mean the world — it genuinely helps other people find us for theirs. [review link]  Grazie! — Ana-Maria"

Even 8–10 reviews containing words like *function, event, party, wedding, private dining, venue* will measurably lift you for those searches. **Reply to every review** within 48h in Franco's warm voice.

> Get your short review link from your profile ("Ask for reviews" → copy link, looks like `g.page/r/…`). Turn it into a QR code on the table/bill.
