Good to know

Menu FAQs

Do you cater for dietary requirements?

Absolutely — vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free and most allergies are well looked after. Just let us know when you book or order.

Is the pasta made in-house?

It is — we roll our pasta fresh in the open kitchen upstairs. You can watch it happen.

Can I see the menu before I visit?

Yes — our food menu and drinks menu are both online to browse.

Do you have a cocktail and drinks menu?

We do — Negronis, spritzes, classic cocktails and a tidy Italian wine list, led by our Spritz & Negroni Bar.

Do the menus change?

Yes — our menus move with the seasons, so there's always something new to try.
